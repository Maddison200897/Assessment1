After doing the test but I am loyal, trustworthy, organised, work well in teams, good at connecting with others, and more. I believe these are good traits to have in a team. I will always be fair with workload; I have good time management and I am natural always organised. If there is one thing my current boss says about me, it is my organisation skills are through the roof.

How should you take this into account when forming a team?

Going into a team I need to be aware of my weaknesses and how to identify them, have strategies to control them e.g., breathing control, positive mantra, and the method I use at work now ‘think then respond not respond then think’. Be open with your team about your weaknesses so all can be respectful to one another and understanding. Use your team to their strengthens, teams will work better if we are all set up to succeed.
